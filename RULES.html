RULES

ArguVista - Shaping Perspectives Together

An online debate session for people who are afraid to participate in debate sessions out in public. We provide an easy and competitive atmosphere on the website where people can debate in their favorite category without having stage fear.

'RULES'

Respectful Discourse:

Users must engage in civil and respectful discussions. Personal attacks, offensive language, or any form of harassment will not be tolerated.

Topic Relevance:

All discussions should be relevant to the designated categories and topics of the forum. Off-topic posts may be removed.

No Hate Speech:

Hate speech, discrimination, or any form of harmful content targeting individuals or groups based on race, gender, religion, or other characteristics is strictly prohibited.

Credible Sources:

Users are encouraged to support their arguments with credible sources. Misinformation and spreading false information are not allowed.

Multiple Accounts:

Each user is allowed one account. Creating multiple accounts for deceptive purposes is against the rules.

Moderator Respect:

Users must respect and comply with the instructions of moderators. Disruptive behavior or failure to follow moderator guidance may result in consequences.

Privacy:

Users should respect the privacy of others. Sharing personal information without consent is strictly prohibited.

Relevance in Responses:

Responses should contribute meaningfully to the ongoing discussion. Spamming, excessive self-promotion, or irrelevant content will be moderated.

Civility in Debates:

Debates should be conducted with a focus on the issues rather than personal attacks. Disparaging remarks about opponents are not allowed.

Warning System:

A three-strike warning system may be employed for rule violations. Repeated offenses may result in temporary or permanent suspension from the forum.

Appeals Process:

Users have the right to appeal moderation decisions. Appeals should be submitted following the designated process outlined by the forum.

Updates to Rules:

The forum reserves the right to update rules as needed. Users are responsible for staying informed about any changes. By participating in the debate forum, users agree to abide by these rules and regulations. Failure to comply may result in penalties ranging from warnings to account suspension or permanent banishment.
